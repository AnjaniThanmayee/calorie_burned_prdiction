Exercise and Activities

Types of Exercises

  • Cardio: Running, cycling, swimming.
  • Strength Training: Weight lifting, resistance band exercises.
  • Flexibility: Yoga, Pilates, stretching exercises.
  • Balance: balance board exercises.

Tips

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables.
  • Get regular exercise to maintain cardiovascular health.
  • Prioritize sleep to help your body recover and perform at its best.
  • Manage stress through mindfulness, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.
