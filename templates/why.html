Why to Track Your Calorie Burn?

Calorie tracking helps you understand how much energy you are expending through various activities. This information can be crucial for:

  • Weight management: Knowing your calorie burn helps balance calorie intake and expenditure.
  • Setting fitness goals: Track your progress and adjust your workout intensity and duration accordingly.
  • Optimizing workouts: Identify which activities burn the most calories and maximize your workout efficiency.

Suggestions

Mix different types of exercises to create a balanced fitness routine. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity each week, along with muscle-strengthening activities on 2 or more days a week.
